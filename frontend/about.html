About StaySphere

StaySphere is a modern Hostel & PG Management System designed to simplify the management of rooms, tenants, rent, agreements, maintenance, visitors, attendance, expenses, and notices through a secure and user-friendly platform.

Manage rooms, tenants, rent, agreements, maintenance, visitors, attendance, expenses, and notices—all from one secure platform.

Our Vision

Our goal is to provide a reliable, secure, and user-friendly platform that makes hostel and PG management easier for administrators while improving the living experience for tenants.
